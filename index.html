The idea

Most communication is designed to reach as many people as possible.

A Scrollsite is designed to reach exactly one.

The frame of a conversation is set before it begins. Before anyone enters the room, before a word is spoken, before a slide is shown — both parties have already formed an impression. A Scrollsite is built to control that frame intentionally.

It is not a pitch deck. It is not a brochure. It is not a templated outreach message. It is a designed experience that exists for one person, for one moment, in one conversation. Then it has done its job.
