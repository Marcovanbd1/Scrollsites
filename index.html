scrollsites.org — A new communication format

A webpage designed for an audience of one.

Every website ever built was designed for many eyes. A Scrollsite is the opposite. It is a single page made for a single person — to arrive at a conversation oriented, prepared, and ready.

001

The idea

Most communication is designed to reach as many people as possible.

A Scrollsite is designed to reach exactly one.

Two sides of the same moment

For the sender

You control the frame before the conversation begins. You decide what they feel, what they know, what they're curious about — before a single word is spoken. You arrive as the most intentional person in the room.

For the recipient

You feel seen. Someone researched you, thought about you, built something just for you. In a world of mass communication, that is extraordinary. You arrive oriented rather than cold. The meeting starts three steps ahead.

The brief behind every Scrollsite

Four moments

01

Pre-meeting frame

Send it the night before. They arrive already understanding the shape of the conversation. No cold starts. No wasted first fifteen minutes.

02

In-meeting guide

A living document you navigate together. Not a deck with bullets — a designed experience that holds the room and moves it forward.

03

Post-meeting reflection

A record of what was agreed, what mattered, what comes next. Sent after. Something they return to. A gesture of care that continues the conversation.

04

Relationship builder

Not a pitch. Not a brochure. A carefully designed experience that builds understanding across time, one page at a time.
